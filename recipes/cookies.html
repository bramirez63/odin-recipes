Macadamia Nut Chocolate Chip Cookies

Have you been looking for the perfect recipe?

Ingredients

  • flour
  • patience
  • self restraint to not eat the raw dough
  • even more restraint to not eat every cookie that comes out of the oven

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients and print off the recipe.
  2. Pay attention to the quantities and order of recipe!
  3. Double check before adding the ingredient to your bowl.
  4. Take your time.
